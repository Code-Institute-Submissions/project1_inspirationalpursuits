About

Welcome to the Chef and Grandson website! Grandpa Chef hopes to pass on his culinary skills to his Grandson and inspire those who would like to learn and improve their cooking skills. He is a Hainanese and he enjoys sharing his unique cooking recipes on how to make easy yet delicious food dishes from his myriad experiences.

Being a food enthusiast, Grandpa Chef used to be a chef consultant for numerous restaurant chains. With disciples all over the world, he has chosen to retire two years ago to spend time with his beloved grandson. Though he has retired, he is still passionate about cooking and actively experimenting new recipes.
